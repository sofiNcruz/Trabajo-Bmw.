Welcome ToBMW

BMW is a premium car and motorcycle manufacturer of German origin and capital. Its initials mean "Bayerische Motoren Werke" (Bavarian Motor Factory) and its headquarters are located in the German city of Munich. Although BMW initially began building airplane engines and later motorcycles, it is currently best known for its range of automobiles, a market in which it is considered one of the classic German prestige (premium) brands, the main objective of BMW is to minimize the impact of a vehicle on the environment throughout its useful life. Furthermore, the development and production of each new vehicle paves the way for its environmentally friendly and efficient reuse.
